About me

I'm currently a master student majoring in electrical engineering in the University of Pennsylvania. My research interests are soft robotics, soft materials, origami and soro sim2real.

I was born on a small island in Zhejiang Province, China. My interest in the field of robotics can be traced back to watching some Japanese tokusatsu films during my childhood. From a young age, I enjoyed studying various mechanical structures, assembling Gundam models, disassembling and reassembling toy cars, and tinkering with electronic components. Currently, as a graduate student exploring the realm of soft robotics, my research focus lies in several interdisciplinary areas, including soft material actuation, soft robot fabrication, flexible embedded design, and employing machine learning for mathematical modeling, among others.
